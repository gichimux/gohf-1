About Us

We are a Nonprofit organization that is passionate about restoring hope to undeserved youth, women and children from urban slums and rural communities.


Our Mission

To create sustainable social and economic opportunities for youth, women and children who are living in urban slums and rural communities.

Our Vision

To become the premier entrepreneurship and leadership development organization for youth, women & children in urban slums and rural communities.
